The philosophy

The field of 21-cm Epoch of Reionization theory and measurement has always had a strong history of analysis, computation, and precision. Large, statstics-driven code bases have become the standard amongst pathfinders and precursers to the Square Kilometre Array. We want to futher encourage growth and scientific collaboration by bringing together the software developers, the mathematical analysists, and the future users to the Code Exchange (CodeX).
By tagging along with already established conferences, we hope to minimize our carbon footprint.

Who is Invited

  • Software Devs

    Come showcase your code base and gain more users. Tutorials, installation help, community practices ... the floor is yours.

  • Innovative Thinkers

    Have an idea that you want to throw at the community? Come test the computational feasibility of your new mathematical technique.

  • Early Career Researchers & PhDs

    Hit the ground running by spending more of your time analysing and less of your time debugging a new code base.

Format

3 days, 9am to 5pm. Morning tea, lunch, and afternoon tea catered.
A light format with occasional, informal tutorials and discussions.
Can't make it in person? Let us know and we can include you in the tutorials via online participation.
