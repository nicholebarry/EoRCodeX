The philosophy

The field of 21-cm Epoch of Reionization theory and measurement has always had a strong history of analysis, computation, and precision. Large, statstics-driven code bases have become the standard amongst pathfinders and precursers to the Square Kilometre Array. We want to futher encourage growth and scientific collaboration by bringing together the software developers, the mathematical analysists, and the future users to the Code Exchange (CodeX). By tagging along with already established conferences, we hope to minimize our carbon footprint.

Who is Invited

  • Software Devs

    Come showcase your code base and gain more users. Tutorials, installation help, pleas for Github reviewers ... the floor is yours.

  • Innovative Thinkers

    Have an idea that you want to throw at the community? Come test the computational feasibility of your new mathematical technique.

  • ECRs and PhDs

    Hit the ground running by spending more of your time analysing and less of your time struggling with a new code base.
